Dance like no one's watching,
Encrypt like everyone is.

With this motto in mind, Clear Net Security Project (CNSP) moves forward to make the Internet a safer place for everyone. We are an online community that provides free for all articles, software, technologies and much more in the field of web security.

PROJECTS ONLINE

Transparent

From our finances to our code, even this website's, are wholly transparent. This ensures a sense of transparency among the community and brings us closer.

Worldwide

We love interacting with people all around the world. CNSP encourages anyone around the world, reading this, to become a part of the CNSP community.

Coherence

The CNSP community is united, truthful and respectful. We are very welcoming and our doors are open for all, always (if we are not undergoing maintenance :D).

Commitment

The whole of CNSP's community and its staff are commited towards one thing and that is, making the Internet secure for everyone by spreading awareness.
